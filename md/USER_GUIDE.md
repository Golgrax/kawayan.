Welcome to Kawayan AI, the content management and automation platform designed specifically for Filipino Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs).

## 1. Getting Started
1.  **Login**: Use your credentials provided during registration.
2.  **Brand Survey**: If it's your first time, complete the 2-minute survey. This helps the AI understand your "voice" and business niche.
3.  **Dashboard**: Once logged in, you'll see your content calendar.

## 2. Content Management
-   **Content Plan**: The AI generates a 7-day or monthly plan for you.
-   **Post Generation**: Click on any idea to generate a full Taglish caption and image prompt.
-   **Regeneration**: Not happy? Click "Regenerate" (up to 2 times per post).
-   **Scheduling**: Once satisfied, set the status to "Scheduled".

## 3. Support & Assistance
-   **Support Widget**: Click the floating bubble to chat with our AI Support or open a ticket.
-   **Direct Calls**: Premium users can request a live support call directly from the dashboard.

---

## 4. For Support Staff
The **Support Dashboard** is your central hub for assisting users.

### Features:
-   **Overview**: Real-time stats on open tickets, live calls, and support activity.
-   **Ticket Management**:
    -   View list of all user tickets with priority levels.
    -   **Context Sidebar**: When a ticket is selected, you can see the user's business profile and live wallet balance.
    -   **Status Control**: Update tickets to "Open", "Pending", or "Resolved" via the status dropdown.
    -   **Quick Replies**: Use "Canned Responses" for faster assistance.
-   **Call Center**: 
    -   **Live Queue**: See users waiting for a live call and connect instantly.
    -   **Call Logs**: Review history of past support sessions.

---

## 5. For Administrators
The **Administrator Panel** provides full system governance.

### Management Tools:
-   **Overview**: Monitor revenue, total users, active sessions, and transaction trends.
-   **User Management**:
    -   View, edit, or delete accounts.
    -   **Wallet Control**: Manually adjust user balances or update subscription plans.
-   **Audit Logs**: 
    -   Track all system activities (logins, post creations, balance adjustments).
    -   The 5 latest logs are visible on the Overview; use the "Audit Logs" tab for the full history.
-   **System Settings**: Toggle maintenance mode or registration availability.

---

## 6. Account Settings
-   Access **Settings** from the navigation bar.
-   **Users**: Update brand identity, change passwords, and manage billing.
-   **Support**: Manage your agent account credentials and theme preferences.
